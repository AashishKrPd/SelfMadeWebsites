Algorithm

Formula:-
\[GPA/CGPA=\] \[\frac{\sum (Grade Marks \times Credits)}{\sum Credits} \]
Explanation:-
\[GPA/CGPA=\] \[\frac {(GradePoints1 \times Credit1) + (GradePoints2 \times Credit2) + ...}{Credit1 + Credit2 + ...}\]
Example (3 Subjects):-
Marks 89 60 36
Credits 2 3 1
\[GPA/CGPA=\] \[\frac {(9 \times 2) + (7 \times 3) + (8 \times 1)}{2 + 3 + 1}\] \[= \frac{47}{6} = 7.83333...\]
